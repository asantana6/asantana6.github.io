What

My hobby is making music. It's a bit of an ambiguous thing to say but that's because I am oftentimes doing very different things with my music. For example, I like to make rock music, jazz music, and videogame music. I dabble with classical music, and ambient/expeimental music. I love Prog Rock, Punk Rock, and Surf Rock. These genres hugely influence the music I make.

Some of my biggest musical influences are:

When

I tend to make music whenever I have free time. Whether it is in the morning before class or late at night after finishing my homework. I ocassionally meet with friends and make music with them, jamming together with guitar.

Usually I also make music whenever I learn some new music theory either through class, watching a video online, or listening to a song and being inspired by it. This tends to make some of the best music I make. One of my most recent pieces was made right after class and it was inspired by an Uematsu piece that I was listening to. Wrote the entire song in one sitting from 4pm to 8pm. It was an orchestral arrangement experimenting with mixed meter and a brass ensemble.

Where

I make my music in my dorm room. Somtimes in my room at my parent's house. I've also met with some friends to play guitar in the dorm halls and in their apartments.
