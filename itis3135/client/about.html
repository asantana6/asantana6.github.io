About Me

I am a student at East Carolina University and am currently in my second year, working towards a Bachelor of Fine Arts with a concentration in Acting and a minor in Creative Writing. I have been passionate about theater since middle school, and it has been my world ever since. Recently, I have discovered my love for Shakespeare (especially Macbeth) and have joined the club “The Shakespeare Guild” to participate in their productions.
